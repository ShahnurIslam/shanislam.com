Shan Islam
2018-01-23  about 196 words  1 min 

We’re three weeks into January so guess it’s unusual to write a new year post so late into the month.

I didn’t post as much as I’d liked in 2017(4 overall) but I will say I really enjoyed my post on Japanese wrestling a few months ago. Hopefully I can do more this year!

Here are my goals for 2018:

  • Move my site from beautiful Jekyll to Blogdown with a new theme!

Thanks for reading, till the next post!
